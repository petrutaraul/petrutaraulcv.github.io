About Us

The company you can rely on

ARBOTRANS LOGISTIC SRL is specialized in road transport domestic and international goods. Besides road freight (tarpaulin), we also carry bulk aggregates and cereals with dump trucks, as well as passenger transport.
All the vehicles are part of ARBOTRANS own park.

ROAD TRANSPORTATION OF INTERNAL AND INTERNATIONAL GOODS

ARBOTRANS company has a car park for road freight transport, diversified, to meet all the demands of the current market.

Efectuam transport rutier de marfuri generale, paletizate, vrac si marfa de volum, dispuzand de autovehicule de 3,5 tone, pana la autotiruri cu greutate maxima admisa de 40 tone ( 24 tone util).

We carry out road transport of general, palletized, bulk goods and volume merchandise, with vehicles of 3.5 tons, up to for cars with a maximum admitted weight of 40 tonnes (24 tonnes) useful).

All the car park is insured CASCO, and CMR MARFA insurance is issued to the company ASKO, at a value of 1,500,000 euros / vehicle.

Also, all vehicles are equipped with GPS Webeye monitoring system.

ROAD TRANSPORT OF AGGREGATES AND GRAINS WITH AUTOMOBILES

Our company has a wide range of dumpers, starting from 16, to 40 tons (3 axes, 4 axles, tractor head and tipper semi-trailer), as well as for the bulk grain transport.

ROAD TRANSPORTATION OF INTERNAL AND INTERNATIONAL PERSONS

ARBOTRANS company has a wide range of minibuses, starting from 8 - 20 seats, as well as 36 seats coaches, equipped to the highest European standards (new car park, 2014 - 2015).

SERVICES PROVIDED WITH CONSTRUCTION EQUIPMENT

We provide services with construction equipment: front loader, excavator, backhoe, compactor cylinder.
